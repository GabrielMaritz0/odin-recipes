Old Fashioned Potato Cakes

These potato cakes are great with any meal or as a snack. Simple and quick to make with only a few ingredients. Kids of ALL ages love 'em.

Simple and what I wanted. I haven't had any potato cakes in ages, so it's quite a treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the potatoes, cheese, flour, salt, pepper, egg, and butter. Shape into 1 inch thick patties.
  3. Place patties on a lightly greased baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
