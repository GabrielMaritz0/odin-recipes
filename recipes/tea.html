The REAL Long Island Iced Tea

Long Island Iced Tea is a popular cocktail that is served in many bars and restaurants.

It is a mix of vodka, gin, tequila, rum, triple sec, and sweet and sour mix. It is served over ice in a highball glass.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz vodka
  • 1 1/2 oz gin
  • 1 1/2 oz light rum
  • 1 1/2 oz tequila
  • 1 1/2 oz triple sec
  • 1 oz sweet and sour mix
  • 1 splash cola

Directions

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add all ingredients.
  3. Stir well.
